Our Story

After completing his Masters’ in Computer Science, Deep Chand started to work as a hardware support engineer and data entry operator in an Export house. Yeah correct, you are thinking it right and he thought the similar way, and this monotonous job could not keep him in for much longer, and then…

Later in 2010, Deep Chand joined a service-based organization as an Android mobile application developer, that was his fascination and interest always (to develop something from nothing, which can make lives easier) where he was not only limited to development but wore multiple hats from the very initial stage. He was always pro-actively involved in Team Meetings, Client Communications, End to End Project Management. Then, he started working as an IT Consultant in 2013 where his prime focus was on resolving current industry gaps in services delivering quality products and providing good after-delivery support. And, from there started the journey of Startxlabs Technologies, founded by Deep Chand in 2014 and started providing services as true partners for its clients. He was later joined by Ravi Singh in 2016 as the other Co-Founder, and he helped to scale the organization in delivering backend services also.

Startxlabs was expanding, and then we established joint ventures in 2017 with our Sales partners based in Australia, and from then on there is no turning back for Startxlabs as we achieved 60% growth every financial year. During this amazing journey, we have been joined by equally amazing and self-motivated team members who have together only helped the organization grow and become better every passing day and we are eternally grateful to everyone who has been a part of this journey.

Our company always strives to achieve excellence in our final result in a manner to provide the best and exact value for our goodwill and vital clients. Our company leaves no stones unturned when it the topic is about being competitive and maintaining our good footprint in this highly enriched world of technologies.

We are strongly aimed on what we perform for our clients and believe in continuous up gradations, so that we maintain in being a versatile provider of a comprehensive package of web related services in various factors of the market business.
